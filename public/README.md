## Image Guidelines

**Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
**Quality**: High resolution (at least 1200px on the longest side)
**Style**: Natural, warm tones; authentic, candid shots preferred
**Lighting**: Natural sunlight, golden hour preferred
**Colors**: Earth tones that complement the brand palette
